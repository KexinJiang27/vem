Instructions

Today, you will be watching some people play on a game show. They will be betting on a spinning wheel. The prizes that they will receive, as well as the chance that they win, will vary from game to game. Each player is accompanied by another person, who sees what the player wins but does not receive any of the prize themselves. We will ask you to guess how both the player and the observer feel after finding out the result of each game.
